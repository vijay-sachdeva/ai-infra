How to read this pilot. The ledger mixes three record types: campuses (a sited facility, sometimes with disclosed MW), capex programs (country-level investment, no single sited MW — most hyperscaler entries), and one policy program (the EU-wide InvestAI gigafactories initiative). Because of that, MW figures are not directly comparable and should not be summed — see the caveats in the open dataset. Known gap: Ireland/Dublin, the #1 addition for the next version.
